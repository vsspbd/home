About Us

Learn more about our organization, mission, and vision in Bangladesh.

Empowering Through Education & Community Service

Vivekananda Shiksha O Sangskriti Parishad, Bangladesh is a social organization inspired by the ideals of Swami Vivekananda. The council focuses on promoting education and social welfare through various initiatives aimed at uplifting underprivileged communities.

Its motto is to spread knowledge and work for the betterment of society, upholding the values of wisdom, harmony, and unity that Swami Vivekananda championed. Through its diverse activities, the council strives to create a more enlightened and socially enriched society.

Quality Education

Providing accessible education to underprivileged children across Bangladesh.

Community Service

Implementing community development projects to improve quality of life.

Youth Empowerment

Empowering youth with leadership skills and values for a better future.
